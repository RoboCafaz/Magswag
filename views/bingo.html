Team WvW Bingo



How to Play

  • Take a screenshot of a square being filled.
  • Upload it to your favorite image hosting site. (We recommend Imgur)
  • Comment on this week's video with the image, the square being fulfilled, and your in-game handle. (i.e. robocafaz.9017)
  • The first players to make a Bingo by 11:59 PM EST the following Friday will get a fabulous prize!

The Rules

  • All images must take place during the matchup the bingo period occurs in.
  • All weekly submissions must be in by 11:59 EST the Friday the matchup ends.
  • All images must take place on a Borderland, Eternal Battlegrounds, or Obsidian Sanctum. Edge of the Mists posts will not be counted.
  • Squares cannot be fulfilled by your own character. (i.e. any "player using X build" or "doing X" cannot be you.)
  • Prizes will be distributed via in-game mail over the weekend after the match ends.
  • If no Bingo is achieved during the weekend the closest match will receive a consolation prize.
  • Players may submit as many squares as they see fit, but will only receive one prize.
  • In the event of duplicate squares being fulfilled, the first person to submit will receive credit. Others will be given honorable mentions.
  • If Bingo is achieved in multiple ways, the winning rows will receive prizes and ALL other participants will also receive a small gift.
  • Rules are subject to change at any point to Deany Kong's disgresion.
  • Prizes may vary from week-to-week depending on participation and funds.
